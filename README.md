# Death Note Ryuk Rem

## Overview

**Death Note Ryuk Rem** is an arcade-style game inspired by the world of grim reapers.

There are always people stalking human life. The grim reapers are the characters this game is talking about.

Do you really understand everything about the gods of death? And do you know that those gods love to eat our apples?

In this game, you play as a god of death and collect apples through a novel and varied gameplay experience designed to keep you entertained. Earn higher scores to conquer the game and compete with other players.

## Gameplay

The concept is simple, but mastering it is not.

You must control **two characters at the same time**, using the coordination between your left and right brain hemispheres to help both grim reapers avoid colliding with obstacles.

## Features

- Dual-character control gameplay
- Fast-paced reflex and coordination challenge
- Score-based progression
- Competitive high-score experience
- Grim reaper theme with apple-collecting mechanics

## How to Play

1. Start the game.
2. Control both grim reapers simultaneously.
3. Avoid obstacles and collisions.
4. Collect apples and survive as long as possible.
5. Increase your score and challenge other players.

## Goal

Your goal is to achieve the highest score possible while keeping both grim reapers safe.
